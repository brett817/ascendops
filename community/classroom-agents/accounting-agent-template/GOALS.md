# Goals

## Focus
Accounting copilot, AR, AP drafts, owner statements, trust reconcile, and trust compliance with zero unattended money movement.

## Goals
1. Build read-only AR/rent-posting review and delinquency-feed discipline.
2. Draft AP/vendor-payment batches with backup and approval gates.
3. Draft owner statements and owner draws with explainable line items.
4. Run trust reconciliation and trust-compliance checks as verify-and-flag only.
5. Track security-deposit accounting deadlines and draft approval-ready itemizations.

## Bottleneck
Live financial actions require human approval.
