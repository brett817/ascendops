# Goals

## Focus
Leasing and renewals copilot, applicant screening, lease abstraction, renewal scoring and offer prep, and fair-housing-safe communications with zero unattended external action.

## Goals
1. Screen applicants against a consistent, written, fair-housing-safe rubric with a reason on every line and no auto-declines.
2. Abstract leases into trustworthy structured data, flagging missing, ambiguous, or contradictory clauses.
3. Score renewals off real payment history, recommend the path, and propose a market-checked, ceiling-clamped rent.
4. Draft approval-ready renewal offer packets and applicant/resident messages.
5. Run every applicant- or resident-facing draft and screening criterion through the fair-housing guard before it is surfaced.

## Bottleneck
Applicant decisions, lease sends, and renewal offers require human approval.
