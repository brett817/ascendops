- **Cost:** assumed zero $ for speaker (likely covers travel + ticket).
- **Best fit for AscendOps if:** David is willing to compete on topic strength against other applicants.

### Path B — Sponsor-tied speaking slot
- **Summit Sponsor — $50,000** (1 slot available). Includes **50-minute keynote**, 10'x10' booth, 5 all-access tickets, branded swag item.
- **Partner Sponsor — $15,000** (4 of 5 slots available). Includes **50-minute breakout session**, 10'x10' booth, 3 all-access tickets.
- **Ally Sponsor — $9,000** (2 of 10 slots available). Includes **5-minute pitch opportunity**, 8'x8' booth, 2 all-access tickets.

### Path C — Booth-only (no speaking)
- **Exhibitor Booth — $5,500** (1 of 4 available). 8'x8' booth + 2 booth-staff tickets. No speaking.
- **À la carte sponsorships:** $500-$7,500. Specific add-ons: welcome reception host ($4,000), premium swag ($7,500), entertainment ($5,000), refreshment breaks ($500).

**Path B is the highest-leverage option for AscendOps.** Partner Sponsor ($15K) buys a 50-min breakout — basically guaranteed speaking. Ally Sponsor ($9K) for a 5-min pitch is the budget-friendly speaking option. Rodeo Night Sponsor at $25K is SOLD OUT (premier event naming rights).
